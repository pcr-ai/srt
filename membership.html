Membership

Home / Our Mandir / Membership

Become an official member of the Shree Raghunath Temple sangat. Membership supports daily temple operations, gives you voting rights in the general body and connects you to a wider devoted community.

Plans

Membership Tiers

Suggested contribution levels — please contact the office to confirm current amounts and benefits.

Annual

Individual Member

One-year membership for a single devotee. Voting rights at the annual general body meeting.

Annual

Family Member

One-year membership for the household. Voting rights for the head of family, member updates to all.

Lifetime

Life Member

One-time contribution for lifelong membership of the temple sangat. Recognised on the temple's life-members roll.

Lifetime

Family Life Member

Lifelong membership covering the entire household. A meaningful way to support the temple long-term.

Patron

Patron Member

For devotees who wish to support major capital projects such as the dome, new murti installations and expansion.

Founder

Founder Patron

The highest tier — for those who make a transformative contribution to the long-term mission of the temple.

Benefits

Member Privileges

Spiritual

  • Priority booking for sponsored pujas (Satyanarayan, Abhishekam, etc.)
  • Birthday and anniversary sankalp at the temple in the member's name
  • Member-only kathas and discourses by visiting saints
  • Personal prasad on major festival days

Community

  • Voting rights at the annual general body meeting
  • Eligibility to stand for executive committee positions
  • Discounted Cultural Hall rental for member family events
  • Member discount on Raghukul Vidyapeeth class fees
