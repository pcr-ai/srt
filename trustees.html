Trustees & Committee

Home / Our Mandir / Trustees & Committee

Shree Raghunath Temple is a non-profit religious organisation guided by a Board of Trustees and run day-to-day by an elected Executive Committee, supported by dedicated volunteer sub-committees and our resident panditji team.

Governance

Board of Trustees

The Trustees provide long-term vision, safeguard the temple's spiritual and financial integrity, and represent the founding sangat.

Chairperson

Provides overall direction, chairs trustee meetings and represents the temple to the wider community.

Vice-Chairperson

Supports the Chair, deputises when needed and oversees long-term project planning.

Trustee — Finance

Oversees the financial health of the temple, the annual budget and major fund initiatives.

Trustee — Religious Affairs

Works with the panditji team on the religious calendar, ceremonies and ritual standards.

Trustee — Facilities

Stewards the temple property, dome project, expansion and ongoing maintenance.

Trustee — Community

Liaises with member families, sub-committees and external community partners.

Operations

Executive Committee

The elected Executive Committee runs day-to-day operations of the temple, with each role serving a fixed term.

President

Leads the executive committee, chairs general body meetings and is the principal spokesperson of the temple.

Vice-President

Supports the President, coordinates between committees and leads strategic initiatives.

General Secretary

Maintains records, coordinates communications, schedules meetings and manages official correspondence.

Treasurer

Manages day-to-day finances, donations, receipts, vendor payments and the annual financial report.

Joint Secretary

Assists the Secretary, supports event coordination and member outreach activities.

Joint Treasurer

Supports the Treasurer in donation tracking, receipts and financial reconciliations.

Sevaks

Sub-Committees

Specialised volunteer groups run the many programs and services of the temple.

Religious / Puja Committee

Plans the religious calendar, festivals, kathas and works with our panditji team on rituals.

Events & Celebrations

Organises Diwali, Holi, Navratri, Janmashtami, Karwa Chauth and other major celebrations.

Raghukul Vidyapeeth

Runs Hindi classes, yoga classes and youth cultural programs throughout the year.

Cultural Hall

Manages hall bookings, weddings, anniversaries and community gathering logistics.

Communications

Newsletter, social media, announcements, calendar and devotee communications.

Volunteer & Membership

Onboards new volunteers, coordinates seva schedules and manages membership records.

Fundraising

Leads the Temple Support Drive, Dome Project, Navagraha Sponsorship and annual fund campaigns.

Facilities & Maintenance

Stewards the temple property, gardens, parking and ongoing maintenance.

Youth & Outreach

Engages teens and young adults through dharmic learning, seva projects and cultural events.

Join Our Seva Team

All roles at the temple are filled by volunteers from the sangat. If you would like to serve on a sub-committee or stand for an executive role, please reach out.
