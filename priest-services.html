Panditji Team

Trained Priests for Every Occasion

Our temple priests are traditionally trained in Vedic karma-kand and perform all major Hindu rituals authentically — at the temple, at your home or at any venue. To book a service, please fill the request form or call the temple office.

Griha Pravesh

House warming with Vastu shanti puja — performed before moving into a new home or office.

Vastu Shanti

Puja to address Vastu doshas in homes, offices and commercial properties.

Satyanarayan Puja

Auspicious puja with katha — for family well-being and special occasions.

Naamkaran Sanskar

Naming ceremony for newborns, performed on an auspicious day.

Mundan Sanskar

First tonsure ceremony for children, typically between ages one and three.

Annaprashan

Ceremony marking a child's first solid food intake.

Upanayan (Janeu)

Sacred thread ceremony marking the start of formal Vedic education.

Vivah Sanskar

Hindu wedding ceremony performed with complete Vedic vidhi.

Engagement / Sagai

Roka, ring ceremony and engagement rituals.

Antim Sanskar

Last rites and cremation ceremonies performed with full respect to tradition.

Asthi Visarjan & Shraddh

Asthi visarjan, tarpan, pind daan and annual shraddh ceremonies for ancestors.

Birthday / Anniversary Puja

Ayushya puja and special blessings for birthdays and wedding anniversaries.

Navagraha Shanti

Puja to pacify the nine planets and bring balance during difficult periods.

Maha Mrityunjaya Jaap

Powerful chanting for health, healing and protection from misfortune.

Rudra Havan / Abhishekam

Sacred fire offering and abhishekam to Bhagwan Shiva.

Lakshmi & Ganesh Puja

For Diwali, new business openings, and prosperity at home.

Akhand Ramayan Path

Continuous recitation of the Ramayan for family welfare and devotion.

Sundarkand Path

Weekly or special-occasion recitation of Sundarkand from the Ramayan.
