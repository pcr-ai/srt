📚 Raghukul Vidyapeeth

Learn Hindi, Yoga & Indian Culture

Our educational wing offers Hindi classes for children, yoga sessions for all ages, shloka recitation, the annual AAROHI cultural program and more — preserving heritage for the next generation.

  • Hindi Classes (beginner → advanced)
  • Yoga, Pranayama & Meditation
  • Shlokas, mantras & cultural values
  • AAROHI annual cultural showcase
  • Sessions calendar 2025–26 available

Popular Priest Services

A snapshot of frequently requested pujas — see the Priest Services page for the full list and to request a booking.

Griha Pravesh

Vastu shanti and house warming puja for new homes and businesses.

Satyanarayan Puja

Auspicious puja with katha — at temple or at home.

Naamkaran & Mundan

Traditional naming and tonsure sanskars for newborns.

Vivah Sanskar

Hindu wedding ceremonies performed with full Vedic vidhi.

Antim Sanskar

Last rites, asthi visarjan and annual shraddh ceremonies.

Havan & Yagna

Navagraha shanti, Rudra havan, Maha Mrityunjay jaap and more.
