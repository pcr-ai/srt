Cultural Hall Rental

Our spacious cultural hall is available for weddings, receptions, birthday celebrations, sanskars and community gatherings. The hall includes seating, sound system and kitchen facilities.

  • Capacity: up to 300 guests
  • Built-in stage and sound system
  • Kitchen and serving area available
  • Ample parking on site
