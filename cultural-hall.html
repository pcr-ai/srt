Venue Rental

A Beautiful Space for Your Special Day

The Shree Raghunath Temple Cultural Hall is a versatile, beautifully decorated event venue for weddings, receptions, birthdays, engagements, sanskars, satsangs, workshops and corporate gatherings. Hosting your event at the temple combines convenience, affordability and the blessings of the deities.

  • ✅ Spacious decorated hall
  • ✅ Full commercial kitchen
  • ✅ Ample on-site parking
  • ✅ Stage · AV · Mic system
  • ✅ Tables & chairs included
  • ✅ 20% off for non-profits

Amenities

What's Included

👥 Capacity

Comfortable seating for up to 300 guests in theater or banquet layout.

🎤 Stage & AV

Built-in raised stage with sound system, microphones and projector support.

🍛 Kitchen Access

Full kitchen with prep area, refrigerator and serving space for caterers.

🪑 Tables & Chairs

Round and rectangular tables with chairs included in standard rental.

🪔 Mandap Setup

Traditional mandap and decoration support available for weddings (additional fee).

🅿️ Parking

Ample on-site parking for guests, with overflow available nearby.
