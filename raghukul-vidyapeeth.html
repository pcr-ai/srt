Educational Programs Rooted in Indian Culture

Raghukul Vidyapeeth is the educational arm of Shree Raghunath Temple. Through Hindi classes, yoga sessions and youth programs, we help children and adults connect with the language, wellness practices and cultural heritage of India.

Programs

Classes Offered

Hindi Classes

This Vidyapeeth program allows students to get familiarised with the fundamentals of the Hindi language and also imparts our diverse culture and values by teaching them various shlokas. Our expert team of teachers works closely with the children.

  • Levels: Pre-Hindi through Advanced
  • Ages: 5 years and up
  • Schedule: Weekend sessions during academic year
  • Shlokas, culture, values & national anthem
  • AAROHI annual cultural program

योग साधना

Yoga Classes

Traditional yoga asanas, pranayama and meditation for all ages and abilities. Classes focus on physical wellbeing and mental peace through authentic yogic practice held at the temple cultural hall.

  • Beginner, intermediate and senior-friendly batches
  • Adults, children and family sessions
  • Early morning timings (typically 7:00 AM – 9:00 AM)
  • Special workshops on International Yoga Day
  • Focus on breathing, meditation & Surya Namaskar

Beyond Classes

Youth & Cultural Programs

🎭 Annual Cultural Day

Students showcase their learning through performances, recitations and skits at the year-end function.

📿 Shlok & Mantra Recitation

Children learn shloks from the Gita, Hanuman Chalisa and other devotional texts.

🪔 Festival Workshops

Hands-on workshops on rangoli, diya decoration, raksha bandhan and more.

📖 Storytelling

Stories from the Ramayan, Mahabharat and Puranas told in an engaging way for children.

🎨 Arts & Crafts

Cultural arts and craft sessions tied to upcoming festivals and seasons.

🕉️ Yuva Satsang

Discussion-style gatherings for teens on values, dharma and modern life.
