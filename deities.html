Divine Family of the Temple

The temple is home to a sacred family of deities. Click any deity to learn more about their pujas and special days.

Shree Ram Parivaar

The presiding deities of the temple — Bhagwan Shree Ram, Mata Sita, Lakshman ji and Hanuman ji.

Shree Hanuman

The eternal devotee of Shree Ram. Special Hanuman Chalisa recitations every Tuesday and Saturday.

Shree Jagannatha Parivaar

Lord Jagannath, Balabhadra and Subhadra — worshipped with Rath Yatra and seasonal festivals.

Shiv Ji

Mahadev — abhishekam performed on Pradosham, Shivratri and every Monday with Rudram chanting.

Shree Krishna

Bhagwan Krishna — Janmashtami, Govardhan Puja and weekly bhajan sandhya celebrate his leelas.

Ganesha

Vighnaharta — first worshipped in every puja. Special Ganesh Chaturthi celebrations annually.

Shree Balaji Parivaar

Bhagwan Venkateshwara with Sridevi and Bhudevi — Balaji Abhishekam held monthly.

Surya

The Sun deity — Surya Namaskar and Aditya Hridayam recitation on Sundays and Rath Saptami.

Maa Lakshmi

Goddess of prosperity — special Lakshmi Puja on Fridays, Diwali and Purnima.

Maa Saraswati

Goddess of knowledge and arts — celebrated on Vasant Panchami and during Navratri.

Maa Durga

Adishakti — worshipped daily with Durga Saptashati and grand Navratri celebrations twice a year.

Satyanarayan Katha

Monthly Satyanarayan Puja and Katha on Purnima — sponsorship open to all devotees.

Sponsor a Puja

You can sponsor an abhishekam, archana or full puja for any of our deities on auspicious days or family occasions.
