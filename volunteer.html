Volunteer With Us

Home / Our Mandir / Volunteer

"Seva" (selfless service) is the heart of our temple. Everything from daily aartis to major festivals to Hindi classes is run by devoted volunteers from the sangat. There is a place for every age and every skill — come share your time, talent or treasure.

Ways to Serve

Volunteer Opportunities

Puja & Aarti Seva

Help with daily aarti setup, prasad preparation, deity decoration and abhishekam support alongside our panditji team.

Festival Volunteers

Diwali, Holi, Navratri, Janmashtami, Ram Navami — major festivals need teams for setup, food, parking, registration and clean-up.

Kitchen / Prasad Seva

Cook and serve mahaprasad on festival days and Sunday darshan. No experience needed — just willingness.

Raghukul Vidyapeeth

Teach or assist with Hindi classes, yoga sessions, cultural workshops and youth dharmic learning.

Bhajan & Music

Sing, play harmonium / tabla / dholak, or help with sound and audio for bhajan sandhya and kathas.

Cultural Hall Support

Help with hall setup, decoration and coordination for weddings, anniversaries and community gatherings.

Communications

Newsletter writing, social media, photography, videography, website updates and event publicity.

Fundraising & Drives

Help with the Temple Support Drive, Dome Project, Navagraha Sponsorship and member outreach.

Facilities & Maintenance

Gardening, cleaning, minor repairs, parking marshalling and ongoing temple upkeep.

Youth Wing

Teens and young adults — lead seva projects, run youth bhajan groups and organise dharmic learning.

Hospitality & Reception

Welcome new devotees, guide visitors and assist with darshan etiquette on busy days.

IT & Tech

Website, livestream of aartis, member database, online forms and digital donation systems.
