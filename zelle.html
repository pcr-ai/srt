How to send Zelle

  1. Open your bank's mobile app or website and look for the Zelle option (Send Money / Pay).
  2. Add recipient using either of the email addresses above.
  3. Enter the amount and add a memo: e.g. "Satyanarayan Puja", "Annadan Donation", or "General Donation".
  4. Confirm and send. Please notify the temple office afterwards for receipt and acknowledgement.
