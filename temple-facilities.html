Our Campus

Spaces for Worship & Community

The temple campus is designed to serve a wide range of devotional, educational and social needs. Below are the key facilities available to devotees and visitors.

🛕

Main Shrine

The central prayer hall houses Shree Ram Parivaar and the other temple deities. Open for darshan during all temple hours.

🔥

Yagnashala

Dedicated space for havans, yagnas and Mata Ki Chowki — designed with proper ventilation and seating.

🎊

Cultural Hall

Multi-purpose hall available for weddings, sanskars and community events. See rental details →

🍽️

Community Kitchen

Equipped kitchen used for prasad preparation and supporting hall rentals with food service.

📖

Library & Reading Room

Collection of scriptures, devotional literature and children's books on Hindu culture.

🧘

Classroom & Yoga Space

Multi-use classroom for Hindi classes, yoga sessions and youth programs under Raghukul Vidyapeeth.

🅿️

Parking

Ample on-site parking with accessible spaces for elderly and differently-abled devotees.

♿

Accessibility

Step-free access to the main shrine and restrooms for the comfort of all devotees.

👞

Shoe Stand & Cloak

Designated shoe stand and storage near the main entrance.

Visitor Guidelines
Please Note

Temple Protocols

  • Please remove shoes at the entrance and place them on the shoe stand.
  • Modest, traditional attire is appreciated, especially during pujas.
  • Photography of deities during aarti is discouraged; outside the aarti times please ask the priest first.
  • Mobile phones on silent inside the prayer hall.
  • Food and drink (other than prasad) are not permitted in the shrine area.
  • Please supervise children and avoid running inside the temple.

Plan Your Visit

First time visiting? Reach out and we'll be happy to help you plan your darshan or event.
