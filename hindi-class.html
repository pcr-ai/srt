Raghukul Vidyapeeth Program

Connect with the Hindi Language & Indian Culture

This Vidyapeeth program will allow students to get familiarised with the fundamentals of the Hindi language and also impart our diverse culture and values in them by teaching them various shlokas.

Our expert team of teachers will be closely working with the children to build reading, writing and conversation skills, along with appreciation for India's rich heritage.

Curriculum

What Students Learn

Hindi Language

Devanagari script, vowels & consonants, reading, writing, vocabulary, grammar and everyday conversation suited to each child's level.

Shlokas & Mantras

Memorisation and meaning of selected shlokas, prayers and bhajans drawn from the Ramayan, Gita and other classical texts.

Culture & Values

Stories from epics, festivals, traditions, the meaning behind rituals and the value-system that underpins Sanatan Dharma.

National Anthem & Patriotic Songs

Children learn the national anthem (with meaning) and other patriotic songs of India.

Recitation & Public Speaking

Regular practice in reading aloud, poetry recitation and group presentations to build confidence.

Arts & Activities

Drawing, rangoli, festival craft and group activities tied to the cultural curriculum.

Eligibility

Who Can Join

Age & Levels

  • Open to children of all backgrounds
  • Multiple levels — beginner to advanced
  • Children are grouped by reading ability, not just age
  • New students welcome throughout the year

Commitment

  • Weekend sessions during the academic year
  • Regular attendance encouraged for steady progress
  • Annual tuition fee — pay online or at temple office
  • Parents welcome to observe and volunteer
