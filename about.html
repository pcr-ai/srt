Our Story

A House of Devotion in the Heart of Plano

Shree Raghunath Temple was founded by a community of devoted families with the vision of building a spiritual home dedicated to Bhagwan Shree Ram and the broader Sanatan tradition. From humble beginnings, the temple has grown into a vibrant center of worship, learning and seva.

Today, the temple welcomes hundreds of devotees each week for daily aartis, abhishekams, festivals, classes and life-cycle sanskars — all guided by our resident panditji team.

Our Pillars

Mission, Vision & Values

Mission

To provide a place of worship and serve as a spiritual, cultural and educational resource center for the Hindu community of Plano and the DFW metroplex.

Vision

To preserve and propagate the timeless wisdom of Sanatan Dharma and pass it on to future generations through authentic worship and learning.

Values

Bhakti (devotion), Seva (service), Vidya (knowledge), Sanskar (values) and Samaj (community) guide every program at our temple.

Our Panditji Team

Our experienced and traditionally trained priests perform all temple rituals and personal pujas with authenticity and devotion.

Head Priest

Trained in Vedic karma-kand and traditional puja vidhi, available for temple and home services.

Associate Priest

Performs daily aartis, abhishekams and sanskars including griha pravesh and naamkaran.

Visiting Priests

Specialist priests join us for major festivals, Satyanarayan Katha and seasonal yagnas.
